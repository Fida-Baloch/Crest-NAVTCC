Welcome to CREST Olympiads

We are a firm believer in innovation and it reflects in our CREST Olympiads Exams. If you’re bored by answering the same set of questions in the Olympiad Exams that makes you feel like not giving Olympiad Exams anymore, then you are at the right place!

Test your knowledge of subjects concepts by taking CREST Olympiads Exams. The set of questions framed in the question paper requires your innovative approach, creativity, imagination, clear understanding of concepts, etc. to solve the questions in the exam. The main vision behind this is to expand the thinking ability of students to make them capable to come up with futuristic solutions for today’s problems.
